TYC SMP was built by players, for players. We wanted to create a space where the community comes first. Whether you are a redstone engineer, a master builder, or just looking to chill with friends, there is a place for you here.

Server Rules

  • No Griefing: Do not destroy or steal from other players' claims.
  • Be Respectful: No hate speech, racism, or harassment in chat.
  • No Hacking: Use of X-ray, Kill Aura, or Fly hacks is strictly prohibited.
  • No Spam: Keep the chat clean and readable.
  • Have Fun! This is a game, let's enjoy it together.
